# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html), version 2.1.
We are committed to a harassment-free, welcoming, and inclusive experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include showing empathy and respect, accepting constructive feedback gracefully, and focusing on what is best for the community.
Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, derogatory or personal attacks, publishing others' private information, and other conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

Because this project invites outside labs and unpublished data, we add one norm: respect data-use agreements and the sealed, prospective design of the evaluation.
Do not share, request, or use held-out data or unrevealed predictions outside the terms under which they were contributed.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainers at lucas.gillenwater@cuanschutz.edu and casey.s.greene@cuanschutz.edu.
All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and the maintainers will respect the privacy and security of the reporter.
The maintainers may remove contributions, or warn, ban, or otherwise act against contributors who violate this Code of Conduct.

For the full text and FAQ, see the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html).
